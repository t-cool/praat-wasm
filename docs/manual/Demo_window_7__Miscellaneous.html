Demo window 7. Miscellaneous

To see whether any function keys are pressed (during a mouse click or key press), you can use demoShiftKeyPressed (), demoCommandKeyPressed (), and demoOptionKeyPressed ().

To put some text in the title bar of the Demo window, try

demoWindowTitle: “This is the title of my presentation”
