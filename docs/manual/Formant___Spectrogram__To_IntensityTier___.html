Formant & Spectrogram: To IntensityTier...

Determines the formant intensities from the selected Spectrogram.

The intensities at the frequencies of the selected formant are copied from the corresponding positions in the spectrogram. If the selected formant doesn't exist in a particular frame, then a large negative value (< -200 dB) is substituted instead. Because the values in the spectrogram are expressed in Pa2/Hz, the units in the intensity tier are in dB/Hz.
