PowerCepstrum

One of the types of objects in Praat.

Description

A PowerCepstrum is the power spectrum of the log power spectrum. When drawn the vertical scale will show the amplitude expressed in dB. The horizontal scale shows quefrency in units of seconds. It is calculated from the Spectrum by a method described at Spectrum: To PowerCepstrum.
