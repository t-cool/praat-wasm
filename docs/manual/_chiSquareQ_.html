chiSquareQ

A function that can be used in Formulas.

Syntax and semantics

chiSquareQ (chiSquare, df)
compute the area under the χ2 curve between chiSquare and +∞, for df degrees of freedom, i.e. the “statistical significance p” of the χ2 difference between two distributions in df+1 dimensions.
